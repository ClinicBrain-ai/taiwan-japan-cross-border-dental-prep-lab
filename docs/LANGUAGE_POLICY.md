# Language Policy

Use decision-preparation language.

Allowed framing:

- "organizes information for dentist review"
- "helps prepare questions"
- "separates facts, assumptions, and unknowns"
- "synthetic example"
- "expert-review preparation packet"
- "requires dentist or medical professional evaluation"

Avoid framing:

- "diagnoses"
- "recommends treatment"
- "interprets images"
- "tells you whether to wait"
- "tells you whether to travel"
- "second opinion"
- "teledentistry"
- "safe to fly"
- "which dentist is right"

When the text mentions urgent symptoms or warning signs, route to local urgent dental or medical evaluation. Do not provide triage instructions or waiting-safety judgments.
