# Synthetic Data Policy

All examples must be synthetic.

Synthetic data is required because the project is a learning system prototype, not a clinical record system. The goal is to test structure, review language, boundaries, and artifact design without handling real patient data.

Synthetic examples should:

- Be fictional and clearly labeled
- Avoid real names, real dates, addresses, phone numbers, emails, record numbers, image files, insurance details, and travel documents
- Avoid rare or identifying combinations
- Focus on planning structure rather than clinical correctness
- Include uncertainty and professional-review prompts

Synthetic examples should not imply correct diagnosis, recommended treatment, image findings, medication plans, emergency status, waiting safety, or travel safety.
