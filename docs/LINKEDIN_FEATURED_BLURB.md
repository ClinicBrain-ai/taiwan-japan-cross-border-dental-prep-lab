# LinkedIn Featured Blurb

Taiwan–Japan Cross-Border Dental Prep Lab is a local-first, synthetic-first AI-native learning system prototype for helping Taiwanese people in Japan and Japanese people in Taiwan organize cross-border dental care planning information into expert-review packets.

It demonstrates decision preparation, not decision making: symptoms, dentist statements, quotes, treatment plans, missing records, communication barriers, cost questions, and timeline constraints are organized into a Markdown packet for dentist review.

The project explicitly avoids diagnosis, treatment recommendation, image interpretation, emergency triage, waiting/travel safety judgment, teledentistry, and formal second-opinion framing.
