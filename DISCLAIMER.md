# Disclaimer

This repository is a local-first, synthetic-first professional learning system prototype. It is not a clinical product, dental advice service, patient portal, teledentistry service, insurance tool, cost estimator, or formal second-opinion workflow.

This packet organizes cross-border dental care planning information only. It is not diagnosis, treatment recommendation, image interpretation, medication advice, emergency care, waiting-safety judgment, travel-safety judgment, teledentistry, or formal second opinion. Final diagnosis, treatment decisions, and whether it is safe to wait or travel require dentist or medical professional evaluation.

Urgent warning signs, severe symptoms, infection concerns, trauma, uncontrolled bleeding, medication questions, and any uncertainty about safety to wait or travel must be evaluated by local urgent dental or medical professionals.

The repository must contain synthetic examples only. Do not place real patient data, real dental images, identifiers, contact details, insurance records, travel documents, or clinical records in this project.
