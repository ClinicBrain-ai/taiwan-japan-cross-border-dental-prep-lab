# Intended Use

This module is for planning preparation and expert-review packet preparation in a bounded Taiwan-Japan cross-border dental context.

It is intended for:

- Taiwanese people in Japan who received a dental explanation, quote, or treatment plan in Japan and are considering whether to organize information for possible care in Taiwan.
- Japanese people in Taiwan who received a dental explanation, quote, or treatment plan in Taiwan and are considering whether to organize information for possible care in Japan.

The module helps organize:

- Original dentist statements
- Translation or plain-language organization
- User interpretation
- Uncertainty
- Local dentist questions
- Home-country dentist questions
- Timeline, language, cost, insurance, and record-transfer planning factors
- Records that may support continuity of care

The output is a bilingual expert-review packet preparation artifact. It should help non-experts prepare clearer questions and better-organized materials for professional review.

## Planning factors only

Cost, insurance, timeline, and language are planning factors only. The module may help users prepare questions about these topics, but it must not guarantee cost, reimbursement, treatment timing, safety, quality, speed, or outcome.

Final diagnosis, treatment decisions, and safety judgments require dentist or medical professional evaluation.

## Urgent warning notice

This situation should be checked locally first. This project cannot decide whether it is safe to wait, travel, or return home. Please seek local urgent dental or medical evaluation before continuing cross-border planning.
